# 1:1 Academic Tutoring

## Building Confidence, Skills, and Independence

Focused, one-on-one support in a specific academic area: reading, math, writing, or another subject. Ideal for families who want targeted skill-building.

![](/assets/uploads/readingmath_photobanner.png)

### **Reading**

Multisensory, Orton Gillingham informed instruction that focuses on a deep understanding of the written word. I utilize a mix of structured literacy and movement to help integrate the process of reading. 

### **Writing**

Authentic writing experiences that focus on the joy of storytelling. Kids work through the full writing process from planning to publishing, focusing on voice, storytelling elements, spelling, and grammar. Kids walk away with projects they can be proud of. 

### **Math**

Hands-on math learning that focuses on building problem solving, flexibility with numbers, and creativity. I utilize games and authentic uses for math thinking to build a joyful practice. 

### **What's Included with Tutoring**

* 50-minute 1:1 sessions, two or more times per week
* Session planning and preparation
* Progress reports every four weeks
* Focus on one primary academic area 

**Best for:** Families who have a strong school program in place and want supplemental support in one specific area.
