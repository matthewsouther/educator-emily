# Consulting & Professional Support

## *For educators, organizations, and homeschool families*

In addition to my work with children, I occasionally take on consulting engagements, bringing 16+ years of curriculum design and program development experience to the educators and organizations who need it.

![](/assets/uploads/copy-of-untitled-design-1-.png)

My consulting work is intentionally small. I take on a limited number of projects each year alongside my direct work with children. If something below resonates, reach out and we'll figure out if it's a good fit.

* **Curriculum design** — developing units, scope and sequence, or interdisciplinary frameworks for schools, co-ops, or education nonprofits.
* **Program design & review** — helping organizations build or strengthen educational programming, with particular experience in experiential, outdoor, and place-based learning.
* **Professional development** — working with educators and teaching teams to build confidence and capacity in experiential learning, inclusive pedagogy, or relationship-based approaches.
* **Homeschool coaching for families** — help designing your homeschool approach, building a curriculum framework, or figuring out what's not working. Includes Oregon PDP support. For more information see [Homeschool Support](https://educatoremily.com/homeschool/).

### Have something in mind?

Tell me a little about what you're working on and I'll let you know whether I can help. I'm always happy to have a conversation, even if it leads somewhere unexpected.
