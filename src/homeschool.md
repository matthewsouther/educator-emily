# Homeschool Support

## Partnership with an Expert Educator

A comprehensive academic program designed for the full school year. I take on the planning, sequencing, and delivery of a robust multi-subject curriculum: two sessions per week, two hours each. Think of it as a private academic director for your child.

![](/assets/uploads/copy-of-photobanner-3-.png)

### **What's included**

* Two 2-hour sessions per week (4 hours total contact time)
* One trial session before committing to the program
* Full curriculum design and scope and sequence planning across subjects
* Monthly 1-hour family alignment meeting — reviewing progress, adjusting goals
* Weekly brief written update on what we covered and what's coming
* Monthly progress report documenting growth across subject areas
* Initial intake and goal-setting session (included in first month)
* Designed for a full academic year commitment

**Best for:** Homeschooling families looking for a rich interdisciplinary curriculum that adapts to your child's interests and areas of development delivered by an expert educator. 

## Coaching

One time or regular coaching to help you through the challenges of homeschooling. We will discuss what's been working and not working while homeschooling along with academic and social goals you have for your child. 

During our sessions I will guide you through building a home learning environment that inspires joy, connection, and real learning. Possible topics we'll discuss are: how to build an interdisciplinary curriculum, planning that works for your family, fueling your energy for homeschooling, creating an intellectually rich family culture, preparing for testing years, and more. 

### What's included

* 1-hour virtual sessions set up on a weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis
* Session notes, next step plans, and resources to use 
* One in person session to see your homeschool learning space and offer design strategies 

**Best for**: Families new to homeschooling, or looking for support building a strong learning environment that works for their family. 

## Academic Evaluation

Are you feeling stuck or not sure what to do next with your child? Are you concerned about your child’s progress or wondering if they have a learning disability? Let’s meet about your concerns and discuss next-step options.

After an initial conversation with you, I will meet with your child to engage in some fun activities, perform basic skills testing, and collect observations regarding your concerns. We will then have a follow-up meeting to discuss my findings. 

*While I cannot diagnose learning or behavioral differences, I can provide you with recommendations for next-steps based on my experiences working with hundreds of children over many years.*

### What's included

* Initial 1-hour parent's only meeting 
* Evaluation sessions - between one to four 50-minute sessions depending on scope
* 1-hour post meeting to discuss findings
* Written report with recommendations and resources

**Best for:** Families wanting a clear understanding of where their child is academically and ways to move forward.
